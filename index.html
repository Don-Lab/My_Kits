What We Do

Manage Yourself.

Here at MY Kits, we provide an exceptional service that helps small-scale farmers and gardeners manage everyday tasks. MY Kits offer sensors that monitor water temperatures, humidity, location of livestock, and levels of sunlight on the property.

RFID Cattle Tracker

This sensor is available for you to be able to track your cattle throughout your property and to determine whether or not all the animals are accounted for.

Trough Temperature Reader

This device is for you to check the temperature of your water trough.

Light Sensor

This device is used to detect the brightness of an area and determine the right area to place your plants.

Soil Moisture Detector

This sensor is available for you to test the moisture of your soil to determine whether you need to water your plants.

We Keep Your Information Private

We do not store any data or information your sensors monitor.
